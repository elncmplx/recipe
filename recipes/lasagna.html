    cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes,

How to make the lasagna step-by-step
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna (according to the following recipe instructions)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna cool before serving
How to layer the lasagna
  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat and top with parmesan
How long to cook


The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook
in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil
for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for
the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna
rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut
into it.
